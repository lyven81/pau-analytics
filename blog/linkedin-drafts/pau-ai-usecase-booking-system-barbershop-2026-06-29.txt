FRIDAY — Pau AI Use Case Spotlight
Solution: booking-system / barbershop
Published: 29 June 2026

---

Razif runs a barbershop in Petaling Jaya. He has 3 barbers and 8 chairs. Every Saturday, the shop is packed. Every Tuesday afternoon, two chairs are empty.

He knows this. But he cannot shift demand from Saturday to Tuesday — because his booking system is a WhatsApp group and a physical notebook, and it tells him nothing.

The Pau AI Booking System changed how his shop runs.

Here is what it does for Razif every day:

• Checks barber availability in real time when a customer messages to book — no back-and-forth with the notebook
• Applies his pricing automatically: RM25 weekdays, RM35 Saturdays — so off-peak slots become more attractive without him having to explain it each time
• Sends a WhatsApp reminder the evening before, which cut his no-shows by around 30%

Two months after going live, his Tuesday bookings had doubled. His Saturday waitlist shrank to a manageable size. And he stopped losing customers who messaged after 9pm and never got a reply.

The shop runs exactly the same as before. The bookings just do not fall through the cracks anymore.
