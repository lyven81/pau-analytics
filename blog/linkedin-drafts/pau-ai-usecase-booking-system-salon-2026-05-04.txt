Category: booking-system / beauty salon
Published: 4 May 2026

---

Mei Ling runs a beauty salon in Subang Jaya with four treatment rooms.

On a Saturday, all four chairs are full. Her phone rings at 2pm. She's mid-facial. By the time she finishes, there's a missed call and no message — the customer booked elsewhere.

This happened three or four times every weekend. She knew she was losing customers. She just had no way to catch them in time.

Now a customer messages on WhatsApp at 2pm asking about a facial on Saturday. Mei Ling is still in the treatment room. But the booking is confirmed in under two minutes — without her touching the phone.

Here is what the system does on her behalf:

- Checks Saturday's availability in real time and finds an open slot at 4pm
- Quotes RM120 for a 60-minute signature facial and confirms the price
- Sends a booking confirmation to the customer immediately
- Sends a reminder message on Friday evening so the customer shows up

Mei Ling sees the booking in her schedule when she finishes with her current client. No missed revenue. No chasing.

For a salon running four rooms six days a week, even catching two missed bookings a day adds up to RM700 to RM1,000 in recovered revenue every week.
