Anita runs a café in Bangsar. Good coffee, solid regulars — and a growing feeling that three new cafés down the road are pulling her lunch crowd.

She had been thinking about adding a set lunch menu. But she did not know what her competitors were charging, which items were drawing crowds, or whether her pricing was already too high.

Before, she would have spent a Sunday afternoon walking the street, photographing menus, and piecing together a picture from Instagram posts.

She tried Pau AI's research system instead. In one session, she asked three questions:

- "What are cafés in Bangsar charging for set lunches right now?" — she got a price range with specific examples
- "What are the most common customer complaints about café lunch menus in KL?" — long wait times and limited vegetarian options came up repeatedly
- "Which lunch items are performing well in competitor cafés based on recent reviews?" — pasta and rice bowls were trending; sandwiches were declining

Within 30 minutes, Anita had enough to make a decision. She launched a three-item set lunch at RM19.90, included one vegetarian option, and added a 15-minute preparation guarantee.

Two weeks in, her lunch covers increased by 40%. She did not guess. She checked.
