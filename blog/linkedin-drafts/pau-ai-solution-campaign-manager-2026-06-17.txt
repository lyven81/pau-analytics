You ran a promotion last week. 300 WhatsApp messages sent. How many customers actually came in?

Most small business owners cannot answer that question with a number. They have a feeling — it was busy, or it was quiet — but no clear link between the campaign and the result. So the next campaign gets planned the same way, with the same guess about what works.

Pau AI's campaign manager gives you a direct line between what you send and what happens next.

It logs which customers received which offer and tracks who responds, when they respond, and what they buy. It shows you which campaign type — discount, freebie, early access, limited offer — gets the best response from which customer segment. It flags when a campaign is underperforming midway through, so you can adjust before it ends.

You can ask it in plain language: "Which of my last three promotions brought the most repeat buyers back?" It gives you the number, not a spreadsheet.

It is built for retail shops, F&B outlets, and service businesses that run regular promotions but have never known which ones actually work. If you are going to spend on a campaign, you should know whether it paid off.
