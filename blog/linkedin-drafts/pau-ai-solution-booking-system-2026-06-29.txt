Your front desk missed 3 booking calls today.

Each caller waited. Nobody answered. They moved on to the next result they found on Google. Each one was worth at least RM100.

For service businesses — salons, clinics, tuition centers, repair shops — every missed call is a missed booking. And most service owners already know this is happening. What they do not have is a way to fix it without hiring more front-desk staff.

The Pau AI Booking System handles the conversation your front desk cannot.

When a customer messages to book an appointment, the system checks availability, confirms the time slot, applies your pricing rules, and sends a reminder the day before — all in one WhatsApp exchange, without anyone on your team doing anything.

What it handles:

Checks your calendar in real time and avoids double-bookings. Applies peak-hour or weekend pricing automatically. Sends booking confirmations and day-before reminders via WhatsApp. Flags reschedules and cancellations back to your team instantly.

Clinics, hair salons, tuition centers, and service workshops across Malaysia use this to capture bookings that would otherwise go unanswered — especially after business hours.
