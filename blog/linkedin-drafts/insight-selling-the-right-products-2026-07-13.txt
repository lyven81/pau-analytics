Your bestselling products may be your business's biggest distraction.

A bicycle retailer gave us access to 60,000+ product sales records. The brief was simple: find which products were actually driving profit.

The accessories — logo caps, socks, tire tubes, patch kits — were selling in the thousands. The sales team spent a lot of time on them. But when the data was ranked by profit, every single accessory was near the bottom of the list.

Meanwhile, the Mountain-200 series in Black and Silver was responsible for 80% of total profit. Each model generated RM1.3–1.4 million in revenue and up to RM630,000 in profit. The team was not giving these products nearly as much attention.

The business was busy. Just not where it mattered.

Pull up your own product data and rank it by profit — not units sold, not revenue. If your list looks different from what your sales team is focused on, you already know where to redirect the energy.

Check your own numbers. Your 80% might be hiding in plain sight.
