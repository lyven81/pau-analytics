Category: analytics-agent / F&B restaurant
Published: 20 July 2026

---

Faridah runs a nasi campur restaurant in Subang Jaya. Eight tables. Lunch and dinner service. Two cooks and a counter helper.

Every month, she looked at total revenue. Some months it was up, some months down. She had a feeling dinner was the weak point — fewer tables filled, shorter stays, smaller orders. But she did not know which part to fix first.

She started using Pau AI's analytics agent, connected to her daily sales records. The first week, she asked three questions:

- "What's my average revenue per table for lunch vs dinner?" — dinner was 34% lower, consistently across all 7 days
- "Which dishes had the lowest profit margin last month?" — char kuey teow and ayam goreng came up immediately; high ingredient cost, moderate sales volume
- "Which days had the lowest total orders in the past 60 days?" — Sunday dinner was the weakest slot by a wide margin, every week

Faridah restructured her Sunday dinner into a shorter, limited menu focused on her highest-margin dishes. She did not change her lunch service at all.

Within two months, her Sunday dinner revenue was up 22%. She made the decision in one afternoon, based on three questions.
