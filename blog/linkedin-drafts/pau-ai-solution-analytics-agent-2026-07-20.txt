Most business owners check their sales numbers once a week. Usually on a Monday, usually for 10 minutes, usually just looking at the total.

That is not analysis. That is checking the meter.

The margin problem, the customer who quietly stopped ordering, the product category draining your cash — none of those show up in a weekly total. They only surface when you ask the right questions of the right data.

Pau AI's analytics agent is built for exactly this. Connect it to your POS, your sales records, your inventory data — then ask in plain English:

- "Which customer segment had the biggest drop in repeat orders this month?"
- "What's my gross margin on the top 10 products vs the bottom 10?"
- "Which days of the week consistently underperform the monthly average?"

No dashboard to build. No analyst to brief. No report to wait for.

One F&B operator discovered a 15% ingredient cost spike in her second week of using it — something she had been absorbing for three months without realising.

The data was always there. She just never had a way to ask.
