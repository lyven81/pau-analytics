Siti runs a tuition centre in Subang Jaya with 80 students across six subjects.

Every week, parents message to ask the same questions: which subjects do you offer, what are the fees, when are the classes, can we try a trial lesson?

During enrolment season — January and June — her admin staff spend most of their day answering these messages. Some parents wait two to four hours for a reply. A few do not wait at all.

Siti set up Pau AI's expert chatbot with her centre's information. Now when a parent asks about fees for Form 3 Mathematics, the chatbot replies in seconds:

- Monthly fee: RM120 per subject
- Classes: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 4pm–6pm
- Trial class: available in the first week of each month
- Maximum class size: 10 students

If a parent asks about a subject the centre does not offer, the chatbot says clearly: "We do not cover that subject currently. Please call us at 012-345 6789 and our team will help you."

No confusion. No wrong information. No promising things that are not available.

During June enrolment this year, average response time dropped from four hours to under one minute. Her admin staff now spend their time on lesson planning, parent follow-up calls, and the students sitting in front of them.

The chatbot handles the intake questions. The people handle the rest.
