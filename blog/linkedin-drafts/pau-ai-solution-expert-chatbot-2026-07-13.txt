Your staff gave a different price for the same service three times this week.

Not because they were dishonest. Because pricing is complicated — peak-hour rates, package deals, add-on charges, member discounts. One wrong answer and a customer shows up expecting RM80 and owes RM140.

Pau AI's expert chatbot handles pricing questions by pulling from one source of truth — your exact rates, your exact conditions, your exact policies. Every customer gets the same correct answer, every time. At 10am or 11pm.

It does not guess. It does not average the numbers in its head. It reads what you set up and delivers it back exactly.

This matters most for businesses where the answer depends on details: a dental clinic that charges differently for a first visit versus a follow-up, a gym where monthly rates differ by branch, a property agent quoting based on unit type and floor level.

The chatbot handles "how much does it cost?" in all its variations — including the follow-up questions. "Does that include GST?" "What about for a senior?" "Is Saturday the same price?" All answered. All consistent.

No more customers quoted the wrong rate. No more staff apologising for yesterday's information.

Businesses using it: dental clinics, gyms, property agencies, service providers with tiered pricing.
