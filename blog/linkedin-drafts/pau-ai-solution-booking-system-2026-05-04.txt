Your receptionist missed 4 booking calls today.

Each one was worth RM100 to RM150. That's RM400 to RM600 gone — on a normal Tuesday.

It happens because the phone rings during a treatment, a class, or a peak-hour rush. The customer tries once, gets no answer, and books somewhere else. You never know it happened.

Pau AI's booking system handles this automatically. Customers send a WhatsApp message. The system checks your real-time availability, applies your pricing rules, confirms the appointment, and sends a reminder before the booking date. No missed calls. No double-bookings. No manual back-and-forth.

It handles conflicts on its own: if a slot is taken, it offers the next available time without asking you. Peak-hour pricing, package deals, and service durations are all baked into the configuration — so it quotes correctly every time.

Businesses using it: beauty salons, tuition centres, fitness studios, dental clinics, home service providers.

You do not need new software. You do not need to train staff. Customers book the same way they already message you — on WhatsApp.
