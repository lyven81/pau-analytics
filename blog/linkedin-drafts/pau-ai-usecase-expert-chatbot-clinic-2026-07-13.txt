Dr. Lim runs a dental clinic in Petaling Jaya with two chairs and a part-time receptionist.

Her receptionist handles four things at once: booking appointments, managing paperwork, answering phone calls, and replying to WhatsApp enquiries from new patients.

The questions are always the same. How much is a cleaning? Do you do braces? What are your hours? Can I book online?

Each question takes two to three minutes. During a busy morning, new patient enquiries wait an hour or more. Some of those patients book with the clinic down the road instead.

Dr. Lim set up Pau AI's expert chatbot with her clinic's information. Now when a patient asks about pricing at 11pm on a Sunday, they get an immediate answer:

- Scaling and polishing: RM120–RM180
- Composite filling: RM150–RM300 per tooth
- Simple extraction: RM100–RM200
- Hours: Monday to Friday 9am–6pm, Saturday 9am–1pm

If a patient asks about braces — a service the clinic does not offer — the chatbot replies clearly: "We do not provide orthodontic treatment at this clinic. Please call us at 03-XXXX XXXX and we can refer you to a specialist."

No confusion. No wrong expectations. No missed enquiries while the receptionist was in with a patient.

Her staff now focus on the patients already in the chair. The chatbot handles the intake.
